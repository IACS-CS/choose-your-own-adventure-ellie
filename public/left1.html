You turn left...

You continue down the trail to the left, hoping that its the right way. God, you wish someone had given you a map.

The wind in this part of the trail is strong and nips at the side of your face and leaves swirl around your ankles.

Up ahead you see the shadow of a person. Theyre tall and looming, neck tilted sideways as they study you. You cant quite make out their face. The fog distorts exactly what they are but you can tell its a living being, breathing and standing in front of you. What will you do?
